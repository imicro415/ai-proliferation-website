Artificial Intelligence is not merely a technical artifact. It represents a structural shift in power, labor, and governance. AI presents us with a vast array of considerations that we should collectively be making. This includes potential environmental impacts, military applications, intensification of competition between great powers, economic ramifications and even the potential for technolgies to be deployed to continue to serve the interests of a small group of highly powerful interest groups. Artificial intelligence in its current iteration is one that is poised to be deployed only to further the interests of the corporations that develop them and those invested in them. We should tread lightly in the face of this growing crisis.
